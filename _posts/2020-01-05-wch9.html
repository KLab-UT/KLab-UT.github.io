I was very fortunate to attend the 9th World Congress of Herpetology, where I gave a talk on reduced endurance and mitochondrial function in asexual whiptails compared to sexuals study (see Research).

It was an amazing conference, with incredible talks, posters, plenaries, and social events. Listening to amazing science, seeing old friends, and meeting academic heroes made it an unforgettably great experience!
